‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add friends?

Go to your Profile ‚Üí Friends ‚Üí Add Friend. Enter your friend's username or share your invite link!

What are the daily limits for free users?

Free users can send and receive up to 3 riddles per day. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited riddles!

How do hints work?

Each riddle has 3 hints. Free users can watch a short video ad to unlock hints. Premium users get unlimited hints.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iOS: Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Riddle.Me ‚Üí Cancel. On Android: Play Store ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Riddle.Me ‚Üí Cancel.

Can I delete my account?

Yes! Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.

How is the leaderboard calculated?

Points are earned by solving riddles correctly. Faster solves with fewer hints = more points! The weekly leaderboard resets every Monday.

I found a bug! How do I report it?

Please email us at support@riddleme.app with details about the bug, your device, and steps to reproduce it. Screenshots help!
