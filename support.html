Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Riddle.Me → Cancel Subscription.

I lost my streak. Can you restore it?

Premium subscribers have access to Streak Savers. If you believe your streak was lost due to a technical issue, please contact us with your username and we'll look into it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. You can also email us to request deletion.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab → tap the search icon → search for your friend's username → send a friend request. You can also share your invite link directly.

How does the daily riddle work?

A new riddle is posted every day at midnight UTC. Everyone gets the same riddle. Solve it to earn XP and maintain your streak. You have until midnight to complete it.
