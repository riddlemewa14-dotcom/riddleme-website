How do I restore my Premium subscription?
Go to the Premium screen and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Why did I lose my streak?
Streaks reset if you don't solve the daily riddle before midnight (your local time). Premium members get Streak Savers to protect their streaks!
How do I add friends?
Go to the Friends tab and tap the + button. You can search for friends by their username or share your invite link.
How do hints work?
Each riddle has 3 progressive hints. Free users get limited hints, while Premium members have unlimited hints.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.
