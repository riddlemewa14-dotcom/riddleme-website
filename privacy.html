Welcome to Riddle.Me! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Avatar selection and display name
  • Game Data: Riddles sent/received, scores, streaks, and leaderboard rankings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and gameplay statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Riddle.Me experience
  • Enable social features (sending riddles to friends, leaderboards)
  • Send push notifications (daily challenges, friend activity)
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your username, avatar, and scores are visible to friends and on leaderboards
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third parties for:
    • Supabase (database and authentication)
    • RevenueCat (subscription management)
    • Google AdMob (advertisements)
    • Mixpanel (analytics)
    • Expo (push notifications)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Advertising

Riddle.Me displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Free users may watch rewarded video ads to earn hints.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption and security practices. We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)

If you are in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data (data portability)
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Riddle.Me is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for daily challenges, friend activity, and important updates. You can disable notifications in the app settings or your device settings at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Riddle.Me after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
